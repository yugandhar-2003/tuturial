About Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at BVRIT was started in the year 1997 with an intake of 40 in B.Tech. Subsequently the intake is enhanced from 40 to 60 in the academic year 1999-2000, 90 in the academic year 2000-2001, 120 in the academic year 2007-2008, 180 in the academic year 2014-2015 and 300 in the academic year 2015-2016. In the academic year 2020-2021, three new UG programmes Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Data Science and Computer Science and Business Systems have been sanctioned each with an intake of 60. The Department has state-of-the-art infrastructure and computing equipment supported by high-speed internet and wireless networks.

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering is renowned for cutting edge research and for imparting state-of-the-art education. The Department provides an outstanding research environment complemented by excellence in teaching. Our faculty with rich teaching and research experience aims at delivering top class education. It also promotes active industry-institute collaboration by identifying areas of interest and taking part in sponsored research projects and consultancy services.

The major areas of research include Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, Cloud & Fog Computing, IoT, Cyber Security, Deep Learning, Image Processing, Information Security, Networks, Augmented Reality/Virtual Reality/Mixed Reality, Blockchain Technology, Parallel processing and Human-Computer Interaction.

Courses Offered

  • B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (Intake - 300)
  • B.Tech in CSE (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning) (Intake - 60)
  • B.Tech in CSE (Data Science) (Intake - 60)
  • B.Tech in Computer Science and Business Systems (Intake - 60)
  • B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (Intake - 60)
  • M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (Intake - 18)

Prospects and Future of CSE

This has been one of the most lucrative and competitive courses to study since its inception in Engineering Colleges/Institutes and Universities. When India’s computer industry created a mark in the world of business and gained super trust and revenue for the nation it also created a large amount of well paid jobs for Computer Engineering students.

Computer Science and Engineering students will learn about basic engineering techniques, as other engineering students, during first year course. From second year they will be exposed to core computer science subjects including Programming, Advanced Data Structures, Digital Logic Design, Algorithms, Computer Networks, Operating Systems, Web Technologies, Databases and Computer Architecture are few to mention apart from trending subjects like Cloud Computing, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, IoT, Cyber Security, Deep Learning, Image Processing, and Blockchain Technology.

As Computing is extensively applied to almost every walk of life it created massive jobs for Computer Engineering students. At the same time, more demand and application kept it research funding alive attracting many students to opt for higher education.

Core companies offering jobs to Computer Science Graduates include Government organizations like DRDO, ISRO, ECIL and BHEL etc. Also, there are a huge number of multinational and national software companies which offer jobs. To name few, Microsoft, Google, Amazon, IBM, TCS, Capgemini, Oracle, Cisco, Infosys, and many more.
